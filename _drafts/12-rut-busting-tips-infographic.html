We have a lot of productivity techniques that you can try. You may be able to get more done working in 25 minute chunks using the Pomodoro Technique, or you can try Eisenhower's matrix to separate the urgent from the important. If you're frustrated from long to-do lists, try breaking them down into weekly milestones with the Infinity Grid.

In addition to those techniques, here's an inforgraphic with 12 rut-busting ideas. But remember, don't beat yourself too much if you're not working at your optimal productive self. Some days will be better than others. Just do your best and don't forget to take breaks!
