Want to share a great story with us? Do you have something to say about productivity, task management, collaboration or your experiences with Sandglaz? We are opening our doors to guest blogging.

If you're interested in submitting an article, please email us at the address below. To increase your chances of getting published, make sure you follow the guidelines and tips below. You will get a reply from us either way.

Guidelines:

  • High quality and relevant content: your article must provide value to our readers.
  • Original and exclusive: your article cannot be modified and published elsewhere. You can link back to the article from your blog to let your readers know about it.
  • 300 to 1000 words.
  • You can provide images as attachments. You have to confirm the source of images for credit.

Tips:

  • Unique personal experience is good.
  • Use your area of expertise by tailoring your article to target your specific niche: teachers, students, developers, startups, consultants, freelancers, event planning, etc.
  • Promote your article. When we publish your article, we will also publish a summary about you and a link to your site. Promoting your article on social media sites will further increase your exposure.

What are you waiting for? Email us at blognull@sandglaz.com with your idea or blog post draft and we'll get in touch with you soon.
