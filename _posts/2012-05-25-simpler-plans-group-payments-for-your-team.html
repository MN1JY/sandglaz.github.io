Today we're improving and simplifying the payment process and payment plans for teams.

Pay for a group

When upgrading with a credit card, you can add other Sandglaz users or invite your contacts to Sandglaz and upgrade them in a single transaction.

Team plan is gone

We are constantly assessing our performance for things we are doing well, and things we're not. The Team plan has been causing confusion, and has seen low uptake since our launch. So starting today, no new Team subscriptions will be offered. Instead, teams should upgrade using the new group payment option.

For those users on the Team plan, rest assured, your plan is grandfathered and will continue to work for as long as you keep it active.

Clearer pricing

In addition to dropping the Team plan, the Infinity plan has been renamed to Pro and is still offered at the same affordable price of $6/month or $66/year for each user. There are no changes to Sandglaz Free.
