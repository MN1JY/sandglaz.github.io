We have been receiving lots of good ideas from our beta users and have focused this release on some of the most popular ones.

Labeling your cells
You will notice all your grid cells have bolded labels defaulting to the urgent- important matrix. The columns represent your time dimension from left to right: now-next-later. The rows represent your importance dimension with the most important tasks in the top row.

You can also use your grids in other ways and overwrite your labels by clicking on them and typing. The example below shows a grid shared to keep track of who is bringing what to the next camping trip. The default labels are replaced with the campers names.
