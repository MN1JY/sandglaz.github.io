Update: This program is discontinued as of October 7th.

With our new referral & affiliate program, you can make easy money promoting Sandglaz. All you need to do is spread the word and you will start receiving monthly payments.

How do I spread the word?

There are two ways we track your referrals:

1- From your referrals dashboard, find your own unique referral link. Use this link to promote Sandglaz. You can use the link on your website, blog, twitter, facebook, email or anywhere you chose. When we receive visitors through your link, we'll associate that visitor with your account. If they then upgrade their account (either on that visit, or on a subsequent one), you'll start earning money.

2- Use the invitation form to invite your friends and colleagues. With this method you may only invite people you know. We will also associate your account with anyone you invite, and you will earn money if they upgrade their account.

How much money can I make?

For every customer you refer, you will earn 50% of their first monthly payment and 10% from all their subsequent payments. This means you will earn a recurring revenue for each customer you refer.

If the customer upgrades multiple grids, you will earn money from all their upgrades. This means there is no cap to how much money you can earn from each customer.

How often do I get paid?

Every 30 days given that your earnings are above $20

How do I keep track of my earnings?

You get your own referral dashboard showing the number of referrals, your gross credit, paid credit and your balance.

Awesome, how do I get started?

If you're not already a user, start by signing up for a Sandglaz account. Then, check your dashboard for your referral link.
