How do I sign in using my Google account?

It's easy and fast. Just click on the "Sign in with Google" button, it will redirect you to a Google page where you enter your Google credentials and then you will be redirected back to Sandglaz. This works for everyone; new and existing users alike.

What are the benefits?

You don't have to remember and manage multiple usernames and passwords, and you don't have to re-enter your sign-in information for each site.

How does it work?

Using OpenID

I already use Sandglaz's sign-in, can I add Google's
sign-in to my Sandglaz account?

Absolutely! If the email you are using for your Sandglaz account is a
Google email account, you can go ahead and click the "sign in with
Google account" button. This will not affect your email/password sign-in. You will always be able to sign in with both methods.

I sign in to Sandglaz with my Google account, but now I want to
use a different email address. Can I do that?

Yes. Set a password for your account: sign in, go to your settings (click on your name in the page header and then click on settings), go to the password panel and follow the instructions. You will then be able to change your email address from the settings page.
