Today we're overhauling our pricing structure. After talking to you and listening to your feedback, we realized that we can make our pricing simpler and better.

K.I.S.S. Pricing

We focus on simplifying planning and task management in the most elegant way, so our pricing structure should be as simple and elegant as our product. From now on, we'll adhere to the K.I.S.S. principle in our pricing.

The Free plan now includes all features starting with 3 free grids, and you can get up to 8 free grids by referring your friends to Sandglaz. This means that the Free plan now includes all previously paid features such as Infinity grids, tagging, recurring tasks and SSL! The Pro plan still gives you unlimited grids and is now priced at $8/month or $69/year. If you are already subscribed to a cheaper plan, it will stay the same for you.

As always, we want to hear your comments and questions. Keep them coming so that we can continue to improve and be better.
