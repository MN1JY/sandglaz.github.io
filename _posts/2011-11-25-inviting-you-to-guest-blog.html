Do you have something to say about productivity, task management, GTD or your experiences with Sandglaz? We are opening our doors to guest blogging.

Guidelines:

  • High quality and relevant content: your article must provide value to our readers.
  • Original and exclusive: your article cannot be modified and published elsewhere. You can link back to the article from your blog to let your readers know about it.
  • 300 to 1000 words.
  • Html format is best, but inline or google doc is okay.
  • You can provides images as attachments. You have to confirm the source of images for credit.

Tips:

  • Unique personal experience is good.
  • Use your area of expertise by tailoring your article to target your specific niche: teachers, students, developers, startups, consultants, freelancers, event planning ...etc.
  • Promote your article. When we publish your article, we will also publish a summary about you and a link to your site. Promoting your article on social media sites will further increase your exposure.
