Today, we're announcing a few features that make it easier to invite friends and share grids.

Share with multiple people at the same time

In the past, sharing a task grid was restricted to one person at a time, now you can enter multiple emails in one go. And as you start typing, an email drop-down list of your contacts will be there to select from.

Import your contacts

Instead of having to look-up the email addresses of your friends and colleagues, you can now invite them to use Sandglaz by importing your contacts from Google, Windows Live (Hotmail) or Yahoo. Any contact you invite will conveniently show up in the email drop-down whenever you're ready to share a grid with them.

Two months ago we announced how you can make money inviting friends to Sandglaz, now we made it a little easier to invite them.
