A few months ago, we made a call for volunteers to help us test out offline support. Now we’re happy to announce that Android offline is out of beta!

What this means for you: 

If you have an Android device and are using Chrome or Firefox, you can access your tasks offline. The offline support is read-only, which means you won’t be able to edit your tasks as of now.


Because of the nature of this project, we also got to make some other performance tweaks that we know you’ll be happy about. You’ll notice that it now takes less time for Sandglaz to load and refresh - both on mobile and desktop.

Let us know what you think about the performance improvements and Android offline support. Leave a comment below, or send us an email with your thoughts.
