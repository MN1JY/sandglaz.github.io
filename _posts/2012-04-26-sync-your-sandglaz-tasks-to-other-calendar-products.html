We are introducing a new feature today that allows you to sync your Sandglaz tasks to Calendars like Apple iCal, Google Calendar, Outlook or any product that supports the iCal format.

The feature is quite simple, first obtain the iCal URL (webcal) of the Grid you would like to sync, and then copy and paste the url into the calendar product you are using.

Obtaining the iCal URL

To obtain an iCal url, click on "Generate iCal URL" from the grid's Sharing menu. Do not share the resulting url with anyone. Each person the grid is shared with can generate their own unique url. You can reset the url at anytime.

Subscribing to the iCal feed

Here's how to sync your Sandglaz tasks to Google Calendar, Outlook and iCal. You can sync to any product that supports the iCal feed format.

Google Calendar

From the 'Other calendars' drop down, click on 'Add by URL'. Paste the Sandglaz webcal url in the field provided.

Outlook

From the 'Open Calendar' menu, click on 'From Internet...'. Paste the Sandglaz webcal url in the field provided.

Apple iCal

From the 'Calendar' menu, click on 'Subscribe...'. Paste the Sandglaz webcal url in the field provided.

The iCal Feed

Calendar products like Google Calendar and iCal only sync events (and not tasks) to the calendar. For this reason, the Sandglaz iCal feed is designed to export tasks as events. The feed only includes uncompleted tasks with dates.

  • Infinity Grids: Tasks will be synced to match their due date. If they don't have a due date, they will be synced to the milestone date they are in. Tasks in the 'Later' milestone will not be included.
  • Standard Grids: Tasks will be synced to match their due date. Tasks with no due dates will not be included.

Tasks with due dates will have an alarm of 15 minutes before the due date in the feed, though note that not all calendar products sync alarms.
