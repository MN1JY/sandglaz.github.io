Today we're thrilled to announced that offline editing—our most requested featured—is now available to all Sandglaz users. Whether you are working on plane or have poor internet connectivity while hiking up a mountain, you can add or edit your Sandglaz tasks and all changes will be synced the next time you launch Sandglaz online.

Thank you for everyone who helped us with testing this feature and providing feedback.
