Sandglaz is embracing paper todo lists with a new print layout for your grids.

So what's different?

Your tasks are listed sequentially with clear headers to differentiate each of the boxes in your grid. The printout also includes any notes you attached to your tasks, as well as the due date. If you miss being able to check off your completed tasks using a pen, there are checkboxes for that!

Ok I'm sold, how do I print my grids?

To print any grid, just click the 'Print' menu item from your grid's toolbar and follow the prompts from there.
